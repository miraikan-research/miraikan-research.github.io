About the Event

Our event will be held in-person, bringing together professors, researchers, students, and others working on or interested in accessibility-related projects. It offers a unique opportunity to exchange ideas, collaborate with peers, and have a good time in our facility.

Organized by: Miraikan, Accessibility Lab

Accessibility Information

Live Captions: All presentations will have real-time captions using Zoom’s captioning feature or PowerPoint CC feature.
Presentation Best Practices: We strongly encourage presenters to speak clearly at an appropriate pace to support accurate transcription, and verbally describe any figures, graphs, or images on their slides to accommodate participants with visual impairments.
